About This Platform

Understand the mission, technology, and team behind the Career Counseling Companion

🎯 Problem Statement

Students today face fragmented career guidance, limited self-awareness of academic strengths, rapidly changing technologies, and evolving industry demands. Traditional career counseling is neither personalized, scalable, nor continuously updated. Many students graduate without a clear direction — missing critical skills and opportunities simply because they didn't know where to look.

💡 Our Solution

The Agentic Career Counseling Companion is an AI-powered platform built on IBM watsonx Orchestrate that provides personalized, scalable, and always-updated career guidance to every student — regardless of background or institution.

The platform combines an intelligent AI mentor with structured career tools: skill gap analysis, learning roadmaps, project recommendations, certification guidance, and industry insights — all in one unified interface.

Student Creates Profile
